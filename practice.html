Travel blog : Journey to Cox's Bazar

Intro:

Cox's bazar is the world's largest sea beach . It's secenary is beautiful . Many tourists go to visit it , it's breath taking view is marvellous . My friend made journey there this winter .Let's hear his story... Once upon a time, there was a young man named Hasan who decided to take a trip to Cox’s Bazar. He had heard so much about the beauty of the place and wanted to experience it for himself. He packed his bags and set off on his journey. Hasan started his journey from Dhaka by taking a bus to Cox’s Bazar1. The bus ride was long but comfortable, and he enjoyed the scenic views along the way. After reaching Cox’s Bazar, he checked into a hotel and decided to explore the city. He visited several tourist spots in Cox’s Bazar, including the famous Cox’s Bazar Sea Beach, Saint Martin, Maheshkhali, Inani Sea Beach, Himchari, Chera Dwip, Sonadia Island, Radiant Fish World, and Shapuree Island/Shah Porir Dwip2. He was amazed by the natural beauty of the place and the hospitality of the locals.
